The World's Most Passionate Fan Cities

Liverpool

England 🏴󠁧󠁢󠁥󠁮󠁧󠁿

Home to the legendary "You'll Never Walk Alone" anthem, Liverpool's Anfield stadium creates an atmosphere that literally moves the earth. The Kop end delivers pure magic on match days.

Anfield Stadium The Cavern Club Albert Dock

Green Bay

Wisconsin, USA 🇺🇸

The frozen tundra of Lambeau Field hosts the NFL's most devoted fanbase. Cheeseheads brave subzero temperatures and create an atmosphere of pure Wisconsin pride and tradition.

Lambeau Field Titletown District Local Breweries

Buenos Aires

Argentina 🇦🇷

La Bombonera stadium and the eternal Boca vs River rivalry create the most intense football atmosphere on Earth. This city lives and breathes football passion every single day.

La Bombonera El Superclásico San Telmo

Dortmund

Germany 🇩🇪

The Yellow Wall at Signal Iduna Park is the largest standing terrace in European football. 25,000 fans create a wall of pure sonic energy that reverberates across the continent.

Yellow Wall Phoenix Lake Industrial Culture

Istanbul

Turkey 🇹🇷

Three massive clubs - Galatasaray, Fenerbahçe, and Beşiktaş - create the most passionate football rivalries. The entire city transforms on derby days into a sea of colors and chants.

Türk Telekom Stadium Bosphorus Historic Quarter

Nashville

Tennessee, USA 🇺🇸

Music City lives and breathes country music like no other place on Earth. From the Grand Ole Opry to Broadway's honky-tonks, Nashville fans are music's most devoted followers.

Grand Ole Opry Broadway District Music Row
